# The-Global-Career-Accelerator-An-Intel-Data-Center
This project uses Tableau to analyze sustainability data in the computing industry, focusing on emissions, energy use, and product lifecycle impact. It transforms complex data into clear dashboards to highlight key trends and support data-driven decisions for reducing environmental impact.

**Tools Used**

* Tableau (data visualization)

**Dataset**
* Source: U.S. regional energy dataset

* Contains data on energy production by source (solar, wind, hydro, etc.) across multiple regions and time periods

**Key Insights**

* The Northwest region leads in hydroelectric power due to geographic advantages

* California dominates solar energy production, driven by climate and policy incentives

* Central regions show strong wind energy output due to favorable land and wind conditions

* Renewable energy adoption varies significantly by region

**Dashboard Features**

* Interactive filters for time period (day, week, month)

  Breakdown of energy sources by region

  Trend analysis using line charts for comparison over time
